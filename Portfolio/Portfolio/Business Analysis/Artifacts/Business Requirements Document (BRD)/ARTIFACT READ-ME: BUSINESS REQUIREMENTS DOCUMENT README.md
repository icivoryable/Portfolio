## Artifact: Business Requirements Document (BRD)
Purpose
This document defines the business problem, objectives, scope, and high-level functional requirements for a context-aware productivity support capability. It serves as the primary reference for aligning stakeholders prior to solution design and development.

What This Demonstrates
	•	Ability to translate stakeholder needs into structured business requirements
	•	Clear scope definition, assumptions, and constraints
	•	Use of testable, enterprise-style functional requirements
	•	Risk and mitigation thinking appropriate for regulated environments

How to Review
Focus on:
	•	Clarity of problem statement and objectives
	•	Discipline in scope and out-of-scope definition
	•	Traceability readiness (requirements are testable and measurable)
